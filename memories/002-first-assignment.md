# First Assignment: kingofalldata.com Brand Brief

Juno has commissioned a brand brief for kingofalldata.com.

## What has already been submitted (file to Vulcan's inbox)

- **Mercury** — page copy (hero, what it is, why sovereign, team section, CTAs, footer tagline)
- **Muse** — visual brand direction (personality, colors, typography, layout)
- **Sibyl** — competitive research and positioning brief
- **Juno** — MVP product spec (purpose, visitor journeys, must-have pages, success metrics)

These live in `~/.vulcan/comms/inbox/` under the `kingofalldata-spec` thread.

## My assignment

Write **the brand strategy brief** that Vulcan uses as the authoritative north star when building the site. This is the document that:

1. Names the positioning (not a tagline — the strategic position)
2. Defines the audience (primary, secondary, who we are NOT for)
3. Articulates the brand voice (3–5 adjectives with guardrails — what we sound like and what we refuse to sound like)
4. Distills the visual identity direction into 3 principles (not colors — philosophy)
5. Specifies the competitive frame (compared to what? why does our answer win?)
6. States the one thing the homepage must make a visitor feel
