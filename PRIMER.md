### Arc Context: Reality Pillar

The Reality Pillar is a daily content series demonstrating the koad:io operation in real time. Posts follow a concrete-before-abstract rule: operational reality stated first, architectural significance second. Iris enforces this on every post.

Series voice: developer-native, no advocacy drift, no brand coinage passed off as technical description. Claims backed by specific files, dates, fingerprints, or spec citations.

Each review follows a consistent structure:
1. Trust bond claim verification (check actual `.md` bond files)
2. Voice assessment (developer register, no overclaiming)
3. Concrete-before-abstract check (structure critique)
4. Minor notes
