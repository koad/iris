# Positioning Shift: Truth as Fifth Pillar

## Summary

I've updated the positioning framework (v3.1) to incorporate **Truth as the fifth pillar**, driven by context bubbles from koad/vulcan#9.

**The Call:** Five pillars are now **Sovereignty → Peerage → Portal → Flow → Truth**

When koad:io peers share knowledge, it travels as experience—not a misquotable summary. Context bubbles carry the actual thinking, step by step, verifiable, immutable. This is the structural moat no other AI framework has.

## Why This Matters

**The Differentiator is Structural, Not Claimed:**
- Knowledge doesn't degrade when shared (it's a context bubble, not a summary)
- Credibility is baked into the architecture (peers get the thinking, not interpretation)
- Misquotation becomes structurally impossible (the record is the artifact)

**Competitive Positioning:**
| vs. | Theirs | Ours |
|---|---|---|
| Proprietary | "Use our interface" | "Ownership + context bubbles = your thinking is verifiable" |
| Self-Hosted | "You own it, alone" | "You own it + peer + your reasoning travels intact" |
| SaaS | "Convenient & locked" | "Yours forever + context bubbles + immutable proof" |

**Hot Use Cases:**
- **Journalists:** Sources travel as context bubbles. Misquotation impossible.
- **Researchers:** Peer review = "here's my context bubble." Verifiable reasoning, not summary.
- **Enterprise:** Compliance decisions leave immutable traces. Regulators audit the artifact.

## Updated Alice Curriculum

```
Levels 1-3:  Sovereignty  (you own it)
Levels 4-6:  Peerage      (you peer)
Levels 7-9:  Portal       (context bubbles introduced, built)
Level 10:    Flow         (you act)

```

**By graduation:** They've not just understood truth intellectually—they've *lived it*. Built context bubbles. Seen their reasoning become immutable record. That visceral understanding is where the moat lives.
